FAQ

SEI is a nonprofit aimed to make STEM resources accessible to all students around the world. Whether they are in Middle school, High school, or college, SEI benefits all. With our quality resources anyone can learn STEM easily. Join today to get involved!

SEI covers vast material in all things STEM. By joining SEI you would be gaining invaluable knowledge that you cam apply to school, competitions, and more. In additon, by taking the extra step and volunteering at our organization allows you to make a difference while also being eligible to recive awards such as the PVSA award.

There is a place for everyone at SEI. From volunteering to starting a chapter, you can get involved with SEI multiple ways. By volunteering for us, whether is May be helping out with events or creating resources you can qualify for the PVSA award. In addition you can also make a change in your community by starting a chapter or hosting events.

Follow us on other platforms in order to support our cause. by following you expand our reach and allow us to spread STEM education to various parts of the world.
